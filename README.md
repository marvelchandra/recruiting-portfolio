## Sourcing Depth: Where I Find Talent Others Don't

### Technical / AI/ML / Research Roles

| Channel | What I look for |
|---|---|
| **ArXiv** | Author pages for papers in target research areas |
| **Papers With Code** | Contributor lists on benchmark-leading implementations |
| **GitHub** | Commit histories on major OSS ML frameworks (PyTorch, JAX, vLLM) |
| **Google Scholar** | Citation networks — who's citing the papers that matter to your team |
| **Kaggle** | Top-ranked competitors in relevant tracks |
| **University AI labs** | PhD/PostDoc pipelines at Stanford, Berkeley, MIT, CMU, Caltech, Cornell |
| **OpenReview** | ICLR/NeurIPS spotlight and oral paper authors |
| **Semantic Scholar** | h-index filtering, field-of-study search, co-author networks |

### Semiconductor & Hardware

| Channel | What I look for |
|---|---|

| **Google Patents** | Inventors on recent patents — named, searchable, public |
| **HotChips / ISSCC / DAC** | Presenter and panelist lists |
| **GitHub (HDL)** | SystemVerilog, VHDL, Chisel open-source contributors |
